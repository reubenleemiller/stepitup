Get ready to Step It Up this summer!

The Step It Up summer prep programs are designed to help students from Kindergarten to Grade 6 build the academic confidence they need for a strong start in September. With targeted support in Math and Language, plus practical tips for each grade level, each 6-week program blends learning, skill-building, and fun. Whether your child is starting Kindergarten or preparing for Grade 6, these structured, one on one sessions help reinforce key concepts, develop foundational skills, and make the transition to the next grade smoother. Sessions are conducted via Video Call available in both English and French immersion options, with sibling discounts available.

Explore the packages we offer below, then proceed to payment and booking. If you have any questions, please contact us at info@stepituplearning.ca or call 403-598-4840

General tutoring is also offered for custom learning goals.
