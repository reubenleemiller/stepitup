Cookie Policy

Effective Date: Mon. Jul 28th 2025

At Step It Up Learning, we respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices you have.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, enhance user experiences, and provide analytical information to site owners.

Cookies can be:

  • First-party cookies: Set by our website (https://www.stepituplearning.ca).
  • Third-party cookies: Set by other websites or services (e.g., analytics or social media platforms).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

2.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function correctly. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, secure login, and form submissions. Without these cookies, certain parts of our website may not work properly.

2.2. Performance & Analytics Cookies

We use these cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. This includes collecting information about:

  • The number of visitors.
  • Which pages are visited most often.
  • How users navigate our site.

We may use Google Analytics or similar services to collect this information anonymously.

2.3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences (such as language settings) to enhance your user experience.

2.4. Marketing & Advertising Cookies

If applicable, we may use cookies to deliver personalized advertising or measure the effectiveness of campaigns. This includes tracking user interactions with our ads and content.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may use third-party services that set their own cookies, such as:

  • Google Analytics (for website traffic insights).
  • Social media plugins (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, or LinkedIn buttons).

We do not control the use of third-party cookies. For more information, please refer to the respective third parties’ cookie policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies.
  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Receive notifications when a cookie is set.

To learn how to manage cookies in your browser, visit:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Consent to Use Cookies

When you first visit https://www.stepituplearning.ca, you may see a cookie banner or pop-up requesting your consent for the use of non-essential cookies. By continuing to use our site, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our business practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, please contact us at:
